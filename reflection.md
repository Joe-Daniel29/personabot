# Reflection

This project was built to explore how system prompts can shape the behavior and tone of an AI model to simulate different domain experts.

## What worked
- **Structured Prompts**: Breaking the prompt into distinct sections (Identity, Style, Format) helped the model stay consistent.
- **Specific Output Rules**: Asking the model to keep responses within a specific sentence count prevented overly long, generic answers.

## Improvements
- **Context Awareness**: The bot could be improved by maintaining context across persona switches or allowing them to "collaborate".
- **Dynamic Examples**: Feeding the prompt with few-shot examples could improve the stylistic adherence of the personas.
